      Racquet gripping
      Before learning the different strokes you need to know about your racquet grip. There are differnt grips for each swing that will be covered later on.

        Groundstrokes
        Forehand Stroke
        A forehand stroke is one of the 2 groundstrokes that is sort of like a side swing to hit the ball. The swing is typically done with your dominant arm on your dominant side. Some professionals who play tennis a lot can get tennis elbow making one hand swings hurt so they will hold the racquet with both
        Backhand Stroke
          A backhand stroke is another one of the 2 groundstrokes. It is the same as a forehand stroke except both hands will grab the racquet and the swing will be done on your non-dominant side.
        Volley
        A Volley is a quick punch to the ball that does not touch the ground otherwise it would be considered a groundstroke.
